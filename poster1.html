Physical AI:
Understanding and Building the Physical World

1st Workshop on Physical AI @ ECCV 2026

Bringing together vision, graphics, robotics, and generative modeling to advance physics-grounded world understanding: from physical property estimation and 3D/4D reconstruction to simulation, physically plausible generation, and embodied interaction.
